Kheto — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 17 May 2026  ·  App version: 1.0

Kheto is a fire risk alert and farming advisor app for Indian farmers. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how it is used. We collect only what is necessary for the app to function.

1. Data We Collect

Farm Location

You pin your farm location during onboarding. We store the latitude and longitude of your farm — not your real-time GPS position. This is used to:

  • Calculate the distance between your farm and nearby fires
  • Fetch weather data for your farm area
  • Determine which fire push notifications you should receive

Farm location is stored on your device (locally) and, if you sign in with Google, also in our secure database (Firebase Firestore) so your settings are preserved across devices.

Crops and Farm Details

Crop type, farm size, and alert radius that you provide during setup. This is used to personalise responses from the AI farming advisor. Stored locally on your device and, for signed-in users, in Firebase Firestore.

Device ID

An anonymous random ID generated on first launch. Used for Firebase Analytics (to count app usage anonymously) and to register your device for push notifications. Never linked to your name, phone number, or email address on the guest path.

Google Account (signed-in users only)

If you choose to sign in with Google, we collect your name and email address via Google OAuth. This is stored in Firebase Firestore and used only to identify your account so your farm settings can sync across devices. We do not access any other Google account data.

Push Notification Token (FCM Token)

A token generated by Firebase Cloud Messaging that allows us to send fire alert notifications to your device. Stored in Firebase Firestore alongside your farm location and alert radius so the server knows which devices to notify when a fire appears nearby.

Chat Messages (Advisor)

Messages you send to the Kheto AI advisor are transmitted to Google's Gemini API to generate a response. Kheto does not store your chat messages. Each session starts fresh. Google's handling of Gemini API data is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

2. Data We Do NOT Collect

  • Your real-time or live GPS location — only your pinned farm location
  • Contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or any other device data
  • Financial or payment information
  • Precise movement or location history
  • Any data from children — Kheto is intended for adults

3. Third-Party Services

Kheto uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

  • Firebase (Google) — Authentication, database (Firestore), push notifications (FCM), and anonymous analytics. Firebase Privacy Policy
  • Google Gemini API — Powers the AI farming advisor. Your messages are sent to Google's servers to generate responses. Google Privacy Policy
  • Tomorrow.io — Provides weather data for your farm location. Your farm coordinates are sent to Tomorrow.io's servers to fetch forecast data. Tomorrow.io Privacy Policy
  • Google Maps — Used to display the fire map and farm location picker. Map usage is governed by Google's standard Maps terms. Google Privacy Policy
  • NASA FIRMS — Fire data is fetched from NASA's Fire Information for Resource Management System. No user data is sent to NASA; this is a read-only data source.

4. How We Use Your Data

  • To calculate fire distance from your farm and display alerts
  • To send push notifications when fire appears within your chosen radius
  • To fetch and display weather for your farm location
  • To personalise AI advisor responses with your farm context
  • To measure and improve app performance via anonymous analytics
  • To improve and develop Kheto's features and services

5. Data Retention

  • Guest users: All data is stored locally on your device. Uninstalling the app deletes all data.
  • Signed-in users: Farm profile and account data is stored in Firebase Firestore. You can request deletion at any time by contacting us (see below). We will delete your data within 30 days.
  • Fire data: Active fire records are automatically deleted after 48 hours.
  • Analytics events: Anonymous usage events are retained per Firebase's standard retention period (up to 14 months).

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your account and all associated data
  • Withdraw consent at any time by uninstalling the app or contacting us

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at the email below.

7. Security

Farm location and account data stored in Firebase Firestore is protected by Firebase security rules that ensure only your authenticated account can read or write your own data. Data in transit is encrypted via HTTPS. We do not store passwords — authentication is handled entirely by Google OAuth.

8. Children

Kheto is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If we make material changes to this privacy policy, we will update the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the app after changes are posted constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
